# WorldWrite Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 2026-06-13*

WorldWrite ("the extension") is a browser extension that helps you translate,
polish, rewrite, and understand text inline using **your own AI provider API
key**. This policy explains exactly what data is handled and how.

## Summary

- We (the developers) have **no servers** and **do not collect, receive, store,
or sell any of your data**.
- The only data leaving your browser is the **text you select**, which is sent
**directly to the AI provider you configure** (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google,
OpenRouter, DeepSeek, or a custom endpoint) to perform the action you chose.
- Your **API key** is stored locally on your device and is sent only to that
provider to authenticate your request.

## What data is processed

**Text you select.** When you choose an action (translate, polish, make
professional, custom rewrite, explain, or translate a received message), the
selected text — together with the action you chose, your language settings, and
any custom instruction you type — is sent from the extension's background
directly to the AI provider you configured. This is necessary to produce the
result. The text is processed transiently and is **not stored** by the extension.

By default (privacy mode on), the extension never sends the page URL, page
title, surrounding page content, full page content, or your browsing history.

**Your API key.** Your provider API key is stored in the browser's extension
storage on your device. It is read only by the extension's background service
worker and is included only in the request to your chosen provider. It is never
written to the web page, never logged, and never included in exported settings.
You can optionally choose to keep the key only for the current browser session.

**Your settings.** Language preferences, selected provider/model, and tone are
stored locally on your device.

## What we do NOT do

- We do not operate any backend and do not receive your text, keys, or settings.
- We do not collect analytics, telemetry, or tracking data.
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
- We do not store your selected text or the AI results.

## Third-party AI providers

When you use the extension, your selected text is sent to the AI provider **you**
choose and configure with your own key. That provider's handling of the data is
governed by **their** privacy policy and terms. Please review the policy of the
provider you use (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, OpenRouter, DeepSeek).

## Data retention

The extension does not retain your selected text or AI results. Settings and your
API key remain on your device until you change or delete them, or remove the
extension.

## Your choices

- Delete your API key at any time from the extension's settings.
- Keep your key only for the current session (do-not-persist option).
- Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored settings and keys.
